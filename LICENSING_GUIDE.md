## Why Apache 2.0?

Apache 2.0 is the license used by Android, Kubernetes, and TensorFlow.
It is permissive, business-friendly, and widely understood. It allows:

- Commercial use
- Modification and distribution
- Private use
- Patent use

With one condition: attribution. You must keep the copyright notice
and license in any distribution.

## Asset Transfer — What This Means for You

ForgeSynapse LTD retains the right to sell or transfer the ClawLite
project as an independent asset to a third party, without selling
ForgeSynapse LTD as a company.

**What this means for users:**
Your rights under Apache 2.0 are permanent and irrevocable.
No acquirer can take away your ability to use, fork, or distribute
the version of ClawLite you have.

**What this means for contributors:**
By submitting a Contribution and accepting the CLA, you acknowledge
that your license grant transfers automatically to any acquirer.
Your copyright is not affected — only the licensee changes.

**Why this matters:**
It keeps ClawLite acquisition-clean. Any company interested in
acquiring ClawLite gets a clean IP chain with no contributor
disputes possible.
